Residensi 22

Residensi 22, Mont Kiara, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Awarded the 2017-2018 Asia-Pacific Property Award under the Category "Best Residential High-Rise Architecture, Malaysia.

Two blocks of 534 units of luxury condominium development -- 40-storey and 39-storey buildings on a 6-acre site, with foundations comprising a combination of precast concrete spun piles, micro piles as well as high capacity footings. The two blocks sit on transfer beams at Level 2, with shear wall structural systems above.

Developed by Sunrise Pioneer Sdn Bhd of the UEM-Sunrise Group.

Completed in 2017-2018.
